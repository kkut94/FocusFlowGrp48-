Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn XP?

You earn 50 XP for every Focus Session you complete. At this time, Tasks do not grant XP, but will in an upcoming patch!

How is my Daily Streak calculated?

Your streak increases by 1 for every consecutive day you complete at least one Focus Session. Don't break the chain!

How do I change my Timer lengths?

Navigate to the Settings tab in the sidebar. You can drag the sliders to adjust the length of your Focus, Short Break, and Long Break times.
